About NGETICH Auto Garage

Founded on principles of excellence and precision, NGETICH Auto Garage has been the trusted choice for discerning vehicle owners in Kenya for over 14 years. Our state-of-the-art facility combines European-standard equipment with African hospitality.

Every vehicle that enters our garage receives the attention it deserves. Our team of certified master technicians brings decades of combined experience, treating each car as if it were their own. From routine maintenance to complex diagnostics, we deliver nothing short of perfection.
